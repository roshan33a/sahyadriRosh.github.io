About Our University

Sahyadri College of Engineering & Management, Mangaluru, was established in the year 2007. The institute is recognized by AICTE, Government of India, affiliated to VTU, Government of Karnataka, NAAC accredited with “A” Grade and certified by ISO. The College offers B.E. Degree in Computer Science, Information Science, Mechanical, Civil, Electronics & Communication, total intake being 720, Post-Graduation in M.Tech with an intake of 162, MBA with an intake of 180, and 6 Research Programs for Doctoral Degree. The total staff and student strength at campus is nearly 4000.
